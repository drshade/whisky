# Compass Box The Peat Monster — 2026-06-01

## Overall

Really nice and smooth. Perfectly balanced — the smokiness is front and centre but never
aggressive; it probably masks some of the complexity sitting beneath. A little bit of
"rotty foot" underneath in a pleasant way — the classic Islay phenolic funk, totally
friendly here rather than medicinal. All-day drinkable.
