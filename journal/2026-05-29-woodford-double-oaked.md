# Woodford Reserve Double Oaked — 2026-05-29

## Verdict
Really good — I'd put it in **similar territory to Blanton's Gold.** Always happy to drink
it: it's **well priced and easy to get**, which makes it a reliable everyday pour.

## Palate
- classic caramel + sweet bourbon profile, rounded by the extra oak

## On bourbon generally (my take)
Bourbons are **generally quite single-dimensional** for me — caramel + sweet and not much
more. Enjoyable, but they don't deliver the layers/complexity I love most (the reason
Hedonism and Kilchoman sit above them). Blanton's Original is the bourbon that rises above
this for me on sheer deliciousness.

## Overall
A dependable, good-value daily bourbon. Caps out where most bourbons do for me — very nice,
not complex.
