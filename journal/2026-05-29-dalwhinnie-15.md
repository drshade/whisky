# Dalwhinnie 15 — 2026-05-29

_Reflective note rather than a fresh sit-down tasting — capturing how my view has
shifted over time._

## Verdict
Liked it when I first bought it. Since then it's come to feel **quite boring and dull**
— gentle and inoffensive but not much holds my attention. The later additions to the
collection, especially **Hedonism** and **Oban 14**, are noticeably more interesting and
rewarding by comparison.

## Why (my read)
Dalwhinnie 15 is one of the *gentlest* Highland malts — soft honey, heather, light malt,
a whisper of smoke. That softness is the point for some, but next to richer, more
characterful drams it can read as flat. Nothing wrong with it; it just doesn't excite me
anymore.
