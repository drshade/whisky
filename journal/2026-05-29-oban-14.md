## Note on that "rotty" undertone
Oban is only lightly smoked — what reads as "rotten/off in a good way" is its **maritime
West-Highland character**: brine, sea-spray and a savoury-smoky edge, rather than true
peat. A genuine, distinctive signature.

## Overall
A solid, characterful mid-shelf dram. Above the bland Highlanders, below the
complex/sweet favourites (Hedonism, Kilchoman, Blanton's Original).
