# Redbreast 12 — 2026-05-30

_Sampled at dinner. This one's on my buy list, so a useful real-world test._

## Verdict
Very good. Nice **sherry flavours** (I think?) — and yes, that's real: Redbreast 12 is matured
in a mix of ex-bourbon and **oloroso sherry casks**, so the sherry note you're picking up is
genuinely there. Good palate.

## Palate
- super pleasant and tasty; sherried; **not particularly complex but really well-rounded**
- vivid descriptor: "like the outer shell of a cracked candy apple" — that glossy, sweet,
  toffee-glazed shell with a tart-fruit hint underneath

## Overall
Lived up to the recommendation. Not the layered complexity of my top tier, but **beautifully
balanced and moreish** — and balance/roundedness clearly counts for me too. Brief dinner pour,
so rating stays tentative; **would definitely try again in a more focused setting.**
**Verdict: buy it.**
