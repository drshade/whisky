# Widow Jane Baby Jane — 2026-05-29

## Verdict
Quite **disappointed**, and not entirely sure why. Possibly because it was **expensive and
hasn't delivered anything meaningful** to justify that — I can't place it any differently
from my other (cheaper) bourbons. Caveat: **I haven't had enough of it to critically
evaluate** yet, so this is a tentative read.

## Palate
- reads like "just another bourbon" so far — caramel/sweet, single-dimensional

## Overall
The premium heirloom-corn story didn't translate into a premium experience for me. Sits
with the rest of the bourbon pack rather than above it — which, given the price, is the
disappointment. **Revisit and re-rate** after a few more pours.
