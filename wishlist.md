# Wishlist

Whiskies I'd like to try or buy. Priority: `high` · `medium` · `low`.
`~Price` is rough market price for budgeting. Move a bottle to `collection.md` once acquired.
💡 = recommended by Claude. Buying budget: R1000–2000/bottle.

## Buy targets

Bottles worth purchasing — sure-thing profile fits and findable picks.

> **SA availability note:** barrel-proof US bourbon (Larceny BP, Stagg, etc.) is largely
> *not imported* into South Africa — don't count on finding it locally. The reliable way to
> get the complexity-at-strength I love is **cask-strength Scotch** (A'bunadh, Glenfarclas
> 105, Ardbeg Uigeadail), which the big-brand SA distribution actually carries.

| Bottle | Distillery | Region | Type | Priority | ~Price | Why I want it |
|--------|-----------|--------|------|----------|--------|---------------|
| A'bunadh (cask strength) | Aberlour | Highland (Speyside) | Single malt | **high** | ~R1300 | Best off-shelf profile match: cask-strength oloroso sherry bomb = sweetness + complexity + earned strength. Fills the sherried-Speyside gap. Buying now. |
| 💡 Larceny Barrel Proof | Heaven Hill | Kentucky | Bourbon (wheated, barrel proof) | low ⚠ | ~R1500+ | Claude pick — BUT **not found in SA** (Heaven Hill's barrel-proof line is US-allocated, rarely imported). Ideal profile fit if ever found; a travel/grail buy, not local. |
| 💡 Ardbeg Uigeadail | Ardbeg | Islay | Single malt (peat + sherry, CS) | high | ~R1400 | Claude pick. Possibly my perfect dram — peat + sherry + cask strength + huge complexity. Taste at NPF first, then buy. |
| 💡 GlenDronach 15 "Revival" | GlenDronach | Highland (Speyside) | Single malt (sherry) | high | ~R1300 | Claude pick. Deep dried-fruit sherry; sweetness + complexity bullseye. |

| 💡 Rittenhouse Rye BiB | Heaven Hill | Kentucky (rye) | Rye | medium | ~R500 | Claude pick. Opens **rye** — spicy, drier counterpoint to my bourbons. Cheap style test. |
